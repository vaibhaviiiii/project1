sphynx

origin: Ontario, Canada

Traits

The Sphynx cat is a breed of cat known for its lack of coat (fur).
Whiskers may be present, either whole or broken, or may be totally absent.
Their skin is the color that their fur would be.
Because they have no coat, they lose more body heat than coated cats.
This makes them warm to the touch as well as heat-seeking.
Sphynxes are known for their extroverted behavior.
They display a high level of energy, intelligence, curiosity, and affection for their owners.
They are one of the more dog-like breeds of cats.
