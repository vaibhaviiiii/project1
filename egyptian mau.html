Egyptian mau

origin: Egypt

Traits

Egyptian Maus are a small- to medium-sized short-haired cat breed.
The spots of the Mau occur on only the tips of the hairs of its coat
Egyptian Maus typically are slender and muscular .
They have anatomical, metabolic, and behavioral differences from other cat breeds which could be considered evidence of antiquity or at least uniqueness from other cat breeds.
The Mau is known for having what is considered a loyal, friendly personality.
